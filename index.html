About Me

Security. Networks. Education.

I'm a cybersecurity and networking professional based in Peterborough, UK, with over five years of hands-on experience spanning network engineering, IT support, and security operations. I hold an MSc in Cyber Security with Distinction from Anglia Ruskin University.

Currently, I serve as an Associate Lecturer at ARU Peterborough and a Lecturer in Computing at Elizabeth School of London — delivering modules in VAPT, Advanced Networking, Web Development, Network Automation, and RDBMS. I'm driven by a belief that great security starts with great education.

My industry background includes network engineering at Jumia PLC, where I managed infrastructure across multiple sites, and IT roles across energy, education, and FMCG sectors.
